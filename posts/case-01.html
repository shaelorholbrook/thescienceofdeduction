Case #001 — The Poisoned Note

Posted by Shaelor Holbrook — 4 June 2025, 19:04

The victim was found in a locked room with a single sheet of paper in his left hand. The note was poisoned. The print was smudged — not by carelessness, but by glove texture. Powder residue on the fingertips. No signs of struggle.

Conclusion: It wasn’t suicide. It was a test. A message. Possibly directed at me. I've already identified the sender.
